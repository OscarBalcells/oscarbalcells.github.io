RedMedic

The POWER of your medical data

Data is the gold of the 21st century. From the improvement and personalisation of treatments, research and the creation of new drugs to the saving of money. hundreds of billions of dollars a year in cost overruns in the health system. Improving the control and use of your medical data can bring extraordinary benefits to yourself, the patient, and the entire ecosystem. Today this information is in multiple silos of disconnected data and it is difficult to collect, organize and share it for the benefit of all.

RedMedic's goal is to break the current paradigm and bring a new approach to the process of exchanging health data. RedMedic applies Blockchain technology, inhackeable and decentralized, to create a system of access permissions, regulated by the owner of the data, the patient, through a digital signature. The medical data reside in the databases of the institutions where they have been generated and are accessible through an open and secure API that verifies petitions using Blockchain.

Technical description

Advantages

Accessibility

A single platform that aggregates the data of the different health care providers that you visit. It offers you a longitudinal health record easier to visualize and share, with data filtered date, supplier and sorted by categories.

Interoperability

Redmedic is a layer that operates above the different data silos that health centers have. It allows the different systems to talk to each other since it uses a format and has an open REST API with authentication for each supplier.

Safety

Decentralization of data leads to many more possible attack vectors with less potential damage instead of a single point of support for the entire system. Authentication of signatures works through secure elliptical curve cryptography (ECDSA).
