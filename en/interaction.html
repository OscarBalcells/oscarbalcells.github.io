RedMedic:   Patient Interaction
The patient interacts with RedMedic through a desktop application. With this app you can perform the following functions:

1) You can view your longitudinal medical history, sorted by categories, date of insertion, and filtered by the provider the particular resource comes from.

2) You can regulate the access permissions to your data by modifying the categories that a certain address can access.

3) Consult the medical history, or a category thereof, of another user in the network to which you have permission to access.

4) Manage different profiles with different digital signatures and addresses associated to Ethereum. A mother can for example manage not only her account, but also her child's through the same application by activating that profile in the profiles tab.

5) Generate a private key, which will be used to register a new user in the network.
